## Method

- **Denominator:** every variation family in the shipped `eternal-return@1.1.0` catalog — 19 families covering 1,014 variations — plus all three endings, audited against the family's concordance mapping and the canonical rule system at manuscript commit `6720e76…` (the accepted literary release's `sourceCommit`; read-only local clone).
- **Sampling (84 items, recorded per family in the concordance `sampledVariationIds`):** all 12 L1 variations; 5 of 81 per L2 family (`-001, -021, -041, -061, -081`); 5 of 45 per character L3 family (`-001, -012, -023, -034, -045`); 9 of 135 conv-L3 fragments (three per `philosophyDominant` framing: accept 001–003, resist 007–009, invest 019–021); all 3 endings in full.
- **Rubric per sampled item:** the Four Shackles (identity/opposition/analogy/resemblance); the four forbidden narrative moves (transmission metaphors, planner/foresight explanations, unified-I after dissolution — scoped to L3/L4 content, transcendence-through-escape, plus the dramatize-never-explain rule); the mapped narrator's voice fingerprint including both cross-contamination blacklists (with designed dissolution-era bleed licensed in L3/L4 and second-person/reader-address framing treated as a permitted edition difference per ADR 0002); chronology/era conformance (near/mid/deep future assignments, slow-bleed catastrophe, no functioning deep-future technology); controlled terminology (Augenblick as rupture, pharmakon dual valence, differentiation/differenciation, protocol completeness per era); canonical names (role-names, "Architect", the supporting cast, no invented proper names); and aptness of the mapping's relationship class, constraint IDs, and promise IDs for what the prose actually does.
- **Designed-repetition allowlist:** the 15 sensory rhymes, phrase bleeding ("I find myself" / "I find myself found"), and the shared sentence "The form is what makes self-observation possible" are never reported as duplication.
- **Verification:** no finding enters the contradiction register unverified. For the five completed family audits, verification was performed inline by the session: every load-bearing quote was grepped against the cited runtime file and the manuscript bible at the pinned commit, with corpus-wide mechanical scans to measure spread (this replaced the original per-finding skeptic-agent design when the owner paused agent-based auditing on cost).

## Verified results (registered as CTR-005…CTR-012)

All five completed family audits assess their mapping's claims as **not fully holding** (`claimsHold: false`) — the relationship class holds in each case, but chronology, promise, and voice claims show real gaps. The two completed L2 audits repeat the L1 patterns almost exactly (era naming, absolute dates, protagonist gender, voice contamination, promise over-claims), and the mechanical spread scans confirm the patterns are corpus-wide (e.g. the algorithm-metric texture `94.7% structural coherence` appears verbatim in nine family files; absolute-year dating in 12 of 15 L1/L2 family files) — systemic authoring-generation artifacts rather than isolated slips, directly relevant to how the #156 content release should be scoped. Registered findings:

1. **CTR-005 (sev-1) — invented era-state facts and era names.** "Upload Era" naming, an Archive under construction in the Archaeologist's era, an operational self-maintaining archive (847,293 consciousnesses) in the Last Human's era against the canonical database failure, the catastrophe recast as voluntary mass upload, and invented institutions ("the Great Archive", "Great Aggregation", "Consciousness Archival Facility 2276").
2. **CTR-006 (sev-1) — absolute compressed dating.** Hard-pinned years (2151/2383/2157/2025 timestamps) and compressed era spacing against the canonical relative decades/centuries/millennia structure; 12 of 15 L1/L2 family files affected.
3. **CTR-007 (sev-1) — Four Shackles identity violation.** hum-L1 declares the three protagonists "single consciousness distributed across temporal positions" in non-dream content; algo-L1 brushes the analogy shackle. This is the concrete fixture case for the 5.2 flattened-reading gate.
4. **CTR-008 (sev-1) — bootstrap paradox explained in exposition.** arch-L1-003 violates dramatize-never-explain at length while correctly avoiding the planner error.
5. **CTR-009 (sev-1) — archaeologist character identity divergence.** Re-gendered female across the audited archaeologist families (internally inconsistent with the ungendered initial) and canonical economic-race motivation inverted.
6. **CTR-010 (sev-2) — concordance metadata errors the audit exposed.** `er-chronology-phase-*` IDs used as movement indices though they denote catastrophe eras, and promise chains claimed with zero presence in fully-covered families; correction coordinates with a slice re-issue because two mappings are slice-pinned.
7. **CTR-011 (sev-2) — algorithm-voice contamination at L1/L2.** Metric/timestamp/hedging texture in archaeologist and last-human prose with no designed-bleed license; nine files carry one metric string verbatim.
8. **CTR-012 (sev-2) — family-internal continuity breaks.** Initial and revisit variations authored from divergent premises (fragment identities, awareness regression, self-contradictory word-count metadata).

## Disposition

Each registered entry awaits the editorial owner's decision: correct concordance metadata (CTR-010 — a metadata change, not prose, coordinated with a slice re-issue), schedule prose revision through the #156 / Batch 5.3 content-release path (line-by-line wording sign-off), grant a Batch 5.2 waiver (rationale + expiry), or accept-as-is with rationale. Open sev-1 entries block the Batch 5.3 gate until decided. No prose was or will be changed by the audit itself.
