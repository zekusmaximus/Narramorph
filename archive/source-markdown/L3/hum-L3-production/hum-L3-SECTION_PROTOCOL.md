XII. COMMON PITFALLS AND FIXES Pitfall 1: Tense Violation Problem: Past tense creeps in ("I walked", "I had examined") Fix: Present tense vigilance, check every verb Test: Search for "walked", "examined", "felt" (past forms)

Pitfall 2: Path Philosophy Stated Not Enacted Problem: "I accept the interface" instead of communion shown Fix: Demonstrate philosophy through sensory experience Test: Remove explicit philosophy statements, ensure stance clear from actions/sensations

Pitfall 3: Awareness Miscalibrated Problem: Low awareness variation has high-level meta-commentary Fix: Check awareness calibration guide, match examples Test: Count meta-commentary instances, verify reader address absent (if not H)

Pitfall 4: Voice Bleed Problem: "Verification protocols revealed..." or "Stream-7 processing..." in hum section Fix: Filter all cross-character content through embodied language Test: Read aloud. Does it sound like Archaeologist or Algorithm at any point?

Pitfall 5: Missing Physical Sensation Problem: Paragraph without physical grounding Fix: Every paragraph begins or contains sensory detail Test: Check each paragraph for body/sensation reference

Pitfall 6: Abstract Philosophy Ungrounded Problem: Philosophical discussion without embodied foundation Fix: Ground every abstraction in physical experience Test: For each philosophical statement, identify preceding sensory detail

Pitfall 7: Sentence Rhythm Wrong Problem: All long sentences or all fragments Fix: Mix short declarations with sensory cascades Test: Count words per sentence, verify 50-60% under 8 words

Pitfall 8: Missing Convergence Seeds Problem: Only two of three seeds planted Fix: Explicitly plant all three (preserve/release/transform) Test: Search for all three seed concepts in text
